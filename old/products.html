HMS – Hospital Management System

It is a package for hospitals & healthcare centers delivering healthcare services to both inpatients & out patients. This solution is mainly used to plan, support & manage the flow of patients. All the services - medical and administrative - rendered to the patients are recorded during the clinical and administrative processes, supporting detailed billing, financial, and cost-controlling records. Key capabilities: Out-Patient & Inpatient Billing & Collection , Communication with Patients (SMS Reminders) , Registrations , Queue Management System , Barcode Generation for Lab Samples & Reports , Calculation of consulting doctor payments based on rules , Reports: Out Patient Reports, Doctor Consultation, Scheduled Appointments, Patient Waiting Time, Out Patient Statistic Reports
